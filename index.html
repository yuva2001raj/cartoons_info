Oggy and the cockroaches

        Oggy and the Cockroaches is a French slapstick cartoon series created by 
        Jean-Yves Raimbaud and directed by Oliver Jean-Marie. The series chronicles 
        the exploits and adventures of Oggy, a blue cat whose life is constantly 
        interrupted by three cockroaches — Dee Dee, Marky, and Joey respectively — 
        who love to cause mischief within his home.
